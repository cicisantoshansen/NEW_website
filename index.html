About

This is me. I mean "She".

Iraci Santos Hansen (A.k.a Cici) was born in Brazil and has lived in New York since 2019. She is a UX/UI Designer who recently graduated from the Springboard career track and has over a decade of experience in the entertainment industry.

She always had a taste for Art and pursued a BFA with a major in Theatre and a minor in Art Education. As an actress, she learned how to understand, empathize, and communicate the message from the script/play to the audience which is a very similar process to Design thinking.
